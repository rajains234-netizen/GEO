# Report Quality Comparison

## Before Audit Report Improvements
- The original report lacked comprehensive data analysis.
- Presentation of findings was not clear, leading to misunderstandings.
- Recommendations were vague and not actionable.

## After Audit Report Improvements
- Data analysis is now thorough and includes relevant charts for better understanding.
- Findings are presented in a clear format with bullet points and headings.
- Recommendations are specific and actionable, providing clear next steps for stakeholders.

## Conclusion
The improvements made to the audit reports greatly enhance the clarity and usefulness of the findings, leading to better decision-making.
